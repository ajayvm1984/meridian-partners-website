▪ Using the checklist

A 90-minute exercise. An honest baseline.

Complete the checklist with your Compliance Officer or senior IT lead. Score each item green / amber / red. Most firms self-assess at 20–25 green on first pass; very few reach 38.

If your score is in the amber-to-red band and a thematic review or licence event is on the horizon, a 30-minute discovery call will give you an honest read on the gap and the realistic timeline to close it.
